## Instructions

1. Open the email you received after purchase.
2. Locate the Epic Games login email and password.
3. Open the Epic Games Launcher.
4. Click on **Sign In** and choose **Sign in with Epic Games**.
5. Enter the login credentials from the email.
6. You are now ready to use the account.
